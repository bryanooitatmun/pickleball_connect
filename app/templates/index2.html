Coaches typically have access to courts where they conduct lessons. When you book a session, you'll be able to see the training location. Some coaches also offer to travel to your preferred court for an additional fee.

Bring your paddle, comfortable athletic clothing, court shoes, water, and a positive attitude! Your coach will provide the balls and any training equipment needed. If you don't have your own paddle yet, let your coach know in advance, and they can provide one for your session.
